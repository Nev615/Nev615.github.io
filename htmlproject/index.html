Geopolitics

Egypt has been an influential geopolitical power in Northern Africa and the Middle East for millennia, and is still one today. Explore why Egypt's location, economy, cities, and more contribute to its long standing influence here.

History

Few places on Earth can claim to have as well-known a history as Egypt does. Some of the earliest evidence of civilization on earth can be found in Egypt, and it is among the most well-studied of the ancient civilizations.

Famous Locations

Hand in hand with Egypt's ancient history are the many edifices created by its people, many of which are thousands of years old and known around the world. They are marvels of engineering that have literally stood the test of time.

Environment

Egypt's best known natural feature is undoubtedly the Nile River, a natural landmark that both facilitated and was foundational to early Egyptian civilization. However, there is more to explore than just this one river in Egypt.

Culture

While Egypt is perhaps best known for its history, it is important to keep in mind that it is much more than its past. Like all cultures, it evolves and is influenced by its past. Explore what makes Egypt a modern cultural hub here.
